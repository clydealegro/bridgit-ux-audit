Bridging Loan

Personas: P01, P02, P03, P04, P05, P06, P08 1 desktop finding · 1 mobile finding
● 1 High ● 1 Medium
F-BL-01 How-it-works steps confirmed present but benefit framing needs verification ▼
Medium Quick Win Effort: Low P01–P06, P08
Finding
The "How it works" section has 3 steps. Step 1 is "Apply instantly — Submit your application, we'll assess within 24 hours." The subsequent steps ("Get approval" and "Move forward") are named but their body copy was not fully extractable. For the Downsizing Empty Nester (P02) and Separating Homeowner (P04), the steps need to convey what happens after approval — specifically when/how funds are released and what triggers repayment — to reduce process anxiety.
Recommendation
Verify Phase 3 visual review confirms step 2 and 3 body copy is plain-language and answers: "What do I need to do next?" and "When does the loan end?" If not, rewrite to make each step outcome-focused rather than process-focused.
Impact of Change
Awareness → Consideration — increases scroll depth and time-on-page by anchoring the product story in recognisable scenarios.
F-BL-M01 No email contact alternative on Bridging Loan page ▼
High Quick Win Effort: Low P08 — Regional
Finding
Bridging Loan page has only tel: contact links. No mailto: email option. P08 uses this page as a product-level research page before proceeding to Eligibility or Calculator; the absence of an email CTA removes the async contact path from a high-traffic consideration-stage page.
Recommendation
Add a mailto: link to the contact block on the Bridging Loan page alongside the phone CTA.
Impact of Change
Lead generation — recovers P08 abandonment by providing non-phone contact path on core product page.
A/B Hypothesis
Email CTA alongside phone will increase contact initiation from mobile users who prefer async communication, improving consideration-to-contact conversion for P08.
