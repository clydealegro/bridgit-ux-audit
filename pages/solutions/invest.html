Solution: Invest

Personas: P06 — Property Investor 3 desktop findings · 3 mobile findings
● 5 High ● 1 Medium
F-SOL-ALL-01 Testimonials are generic across all solution pages; not scenario-specific ▼
High Medium Effort Effort: Medium All Personas
Finding
All 7 solution pages include a Trustpilot/testimonial widget section reused across pages without scenario-specific customisation. A user on the Invest page should see a testimonial from a property investor who used Bridgit to move quickly on a new acquisition — generic testimonials don't speak to P06's analytical mindset or portfolio context.
Recommendation
Source and display at least one scenario-specific testimonial per solution page. For Invest, format as: customer name, scenario label (e.g., "Alex, Property Investor — Perth"), 1–2 sentence quote referencing speed of approval and investor-specific outcome.
Impact of Change
Consideration → Apply — increases apply rate across all solution pages by replacing generic testimonials with scenario-matched social proof.
F-SOL-ALL-02 No worked examples with real dollar amounts on any solution page ▼
High Medium Effort Effort: Medium All Personas
Finding
No solution page contains a worked scenario with concrete numbers. P06 (Property Investor, Financial Services background) is highly analytical and will not proceed without a worked cost model. The absence of investor-specific numbers is a primary conversion barrier for this persona.
Recommendation
Add a worked investor-specific example: "You own an investment property worth $800K (owe $350K). You've found a new acquisition at $700K. Bridgit can bridge up to $290K at 7.54% p.a. — here's the cost breakdown." Include investor-specific rate, LVR, and total cost at settlement.
Impact of Change
Consideration → Apply — reduces uncertainty-driven abandonment by grounding each solution in a concrete cost or outcome example.
F-SOL-INV-01 No investor vs. owner-occupier pricing distinction on Invest page ▼
High Medium Effort Effort: Medium P06 — Investor
Finding
The Invest page does not surface that investor property bridging loans have different pricing from owner-occupier loans (7.54% vs. 7.04% per annum confirmed on Rates & Fees page). P06 (Property Investor, Financial Services background) is analytically driven and will want to understand their specific rate before committing. Without this signal, they must navigate to Rates & Fees to determine the cost difference — or they may assume the headline PPOR rate applies.
Recommendation
Add a rates callout on the Invest page: "Investor property bridging rates from 7.54% p.a. — view full rates." with link to /rates-and-fees.
Impact of Change
Consideration → Apply — increases P06 apply rate by addressing rental income treatment and LVR specifics.
F-SOL-INV-M01 Investor-specific rates and LVR information requires scrolling ▼
High Quick Win Effort: Medium P06 — Investor
Finding
P06 (time-poor, efficiency-focused) needs investor-specific criteria (LVR up to 80%, investor vs owner-occupier rate distinction) visible within the first scroll. This information is not confirmed above fold at 390px. P06 will not invest time scrolling to find basic eligibility data — they will abandon and search elsewhere.
Recommendation
Move a micro-section "Investor details" above the first scroll point (immediately post-hero): "LVR up to 80% | Investor rates available | [View Rates & Fees →]". Keep it scannable — 2 lines max on mobile.
Impact of Change
Consideration → Apply — increases P06 apply velocity by surfacing investor LVR and rate info without scrolling.
A/B Hypothesis
Surfacing investor LVR and rate info above fold will reduce scroll depth and increase time-to-apply velocity for P06.
F-SOL-INV-M02 "Rates & Fees" link not clearly accessible on mobile ▼
High Quick Win Effort: Low P06 — Investor
Finding
No prominent "Investor Rates & Fees" link is visible in the hero or immediate post-hero zone at 390px. P06 needs to verify investor-specific costs as part of their due diligence before applying. The absence of a visible rates link on a product page designed for the most financially sophisticated persona creates a trust gap.
Recommendation
Add a "Investor Rates & Fees" link (labelled specifically for investors, not the generic "Rates") in the hero section or the post-hero utility strip. Minimum 44px tap target.
Impact of Change
Consideration → Due diligence — enables P06 cost research without navigating away from solution page.
A/B Hypothesis
A visible investor-specific Rates & Fees link above fold will increase click-through to the rates page and downstream application rate for P06.
F-SOL-INV-M03 Primary CTA copy is generic ("Apply Now") ▼
Medium Quick Win Effort: Low P06 — Investor
Finding
The primary CTA reads "Apply Now" — generic across all solution pages. P06 operates with a portfolio mindset and responds to efficiency signals. Investor-specific CTA copy ("Apply as an investor" or "Fast-track investor application") reinforces that this product is for their use case. Cross-reference: F-NAV-01 (Phase 2).
Recommendation
Change primary CTA on the Invest page to "Apply as an investor" or "Start investor application".
Impact of Change
CTA conversion — increases P06 click-through by making CTA copy specific to the investor scenario.
A/B Hypothesis
"Apply as an investor" will increase CTA click-through rate vs "Apply Now" on the Invest page.
