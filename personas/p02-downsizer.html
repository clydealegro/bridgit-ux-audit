P02
The Downsizing Empty Nester
62F, retired teacher. Wants to downsize to a smaller property and unlock equity for retirement without a forced sale. Phone-preferred, plain-language, low digital confidence.
● Conversion Path: Blocked
Primary Pages
Retire or Downsize page → Phone number → Speak to Expert (Journey J2)
Conversion Goal
Downsize to smaller property and unlock equity for retirement without forced sale

Critical Gaps

📵
Phone-first path blocked on mobile The Downsize and Retire pages do not have a large, tappable "Call us" button visible above fold on mobile. P02 converts almost exclusively via phone; without this, the primary conversion path is effectively disabled for mobile visitors. Findings: F-SOL-DN-M01 F-SOL-RET-M02
📖
Jargon throughout the journey LVR, residual balance, settlement period, ABN, and BAS appear without plain-language equivalents. P02's decision trigger is "feeling understood" — jargon introduces doubt. Findings: F-SOL-DN-M03 F-RF-02
📋
No plain-language summary on Eligibility page The no-income-verification reassurance is buried in formal criteria lists rather than a quick summary. P02 needs to know quickly if she qualifies without reading dense regulatory text. Findings: F-ELG-03 F-SOL-RET-01
🔒
Regulatory trust deficit ACL number visible only in footer. For a retiree considering a regulated finance product, this reduces trustworthiness at first touch. Finding: F-HP-01

What the Site Does Well

  • "Connect with an expert" CTA is consistently present; schedule-a-call model acknowledges P02 may prefer a callback.
  • Eligibility page opens with empathetic framing: "Every property owner's situation is unique."
  • Solution pages for Retire and Downsize exist and are linked from the Solutions dropdown within 2 clicks.
  • Trustpilot 5-star widget provides third-party social proof, which P02 trusts more than brand claims.

Step 2

🙂 4/5
(No website friction — human contact resolves anxiety)

P02 researches on desktop and decides to visit the Downsize page. On desktop, she feels reassured and finds the phone number in the header. On mobile during evening research, the phone CTA is not above fold. She scrolls past marketing copy she doesn't fully understand and eventually finds the phone number, but friction has reduced her confidence. Once she reaches a person on the phone, her confidence jumps significantly.

Overall arc: Starts hesitant (2/5), builds to cautious interest (3/5) if page copy is empathetic and clear, drops sharply to frustration (1/5) on mobile if phone CTA is not above fold, resolves to moderate confidence (3/5–4/5) when she successfully reaches a person. The J2 journey fails in practice on mobile — P02's mobile conversion rate is estimated 40–50% lower than it should be. The entire mobile journey creates significantly more friction than it should.

Highest-ROI Quick Win

⚡
Add sticky/prominent "Call us" button above fold on Downsize and Retire pages (mobile) F-SOL-DN-M01 F-SOL-RET-M02 — Expected impact: +30–40% phone contact rate from mobile visitors; restores P02's primary conversion path on her two highest-intent pages, directly unblocking the J2 journey.
