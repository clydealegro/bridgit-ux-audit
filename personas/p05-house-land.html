P05
The House & Land Builder
34M, project engineer. Wants to settle a new build on time while selling an existing home without a forced rushed sale. Researches on-site on mobile with variable connectivity. Primary concern is build delay risk.
● Conversion Path: Partial
Primary Pages
House & Land page → Calculator → Eligibility → Apply
Conversion Goal
Settle new build on time; sell existing home without forced rushed sale; understand build delay coverage

Critical Gaps

🚧
Builder delay risk unaddressed The H&L page does not explicitly address "What happens if my build is delayed?" Without assurance that loan terms can flex or extend, P05 self-excludes at the consideration stage — the very question that differentiates bridging products for builders. Finding: F-SOL-HL-01
📱
Mobile is critical and currently under-served Multiple mobile findings indicate above-fold navigation and clarity are blocked on 390px. P05 researches during on-site visits with patchy mobile signal, making fast above-fold loading essential. Findings: F-SOL-HL-M01 F-SOL-HL-M02 F-SOL-HL-M03 F-SOL-HL-M04
💰
Cost modelling friction No worked cost example on Rates & Fees, and a blank-slate calculator without defaults mean P05 must hunt for information to model the monthly overlap cost between build settlement and existing home sale. Finding: F-RF-03
🎯
Generic CTA on scenario-specific page "Apply now" does not confirm P05 is in the right place for a house and land package scenario. A scenario-specific CTA ("Start my house & land bridge") would reduce hesitation. Finding: F-NAV-01

What the Site Does Well

  • A dedicated House & Land Packages page exists, signalling product-market fit for the builder scenario.
  • "Connect with an expert" CTA is available for discussing builder delay scenarios directly with a specialist.
  • Mobile nav passes the 2-tap test, making core pages accessible quickly.
